Who writes Ohio's laws, and what they actually do

Two out of every five items filed in Columbus is a ceremonial resolution, not a law. Of the roughly 1,400 real bills introduced this General Assembly, only 7% pass — and the ones that do mostly patch narrow rules for specific professions and agencies.
