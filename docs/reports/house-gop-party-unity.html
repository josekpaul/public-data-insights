Methodology. Sample covers House recorded votes only (Senate not included). "2/3 Yea-and-Nay" suspension-calendar votes are excluded — they pass near-unanimously by procedural design and would dilute genuine disagreement. A member's "crossover" is a Yea/Aye or Nay/No vote that differs from their own party's majority position on that roll call; vote-cast vocabulary differs between bill votes (Yea/Nay) and amendment votes (Aye/No) and both are normalized here.

This is a first-pass exploratory read, not a definitive study — the 110-vote sample reflects contested recorded votes available via the API at compile time, and rankings may shift as the session continues.
